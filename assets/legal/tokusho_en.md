## Preamble

The App is provided free of charge and does not offer in-app purchases or the sale of any paid goods or services. Except for voluntary donations to support development (made via an external platform), there is no transaction in which consideration is received from users.

Accordingly, the scope of applicability of the Act on Specified Commercial Transactions and whether a full notation (all operator fields) is required will be confirmed by Japanese legal counsel before launch. If a complete operator notation is deemed necessary, it will be prepared separately (planned for v2).

None. The developer charges no fees for use of the App. Communication charges and the like are borne by the user.

## Payment Method and Timing

Not applicable (as there is no paid sale). For voluntary donations, the method and timing follow those specified by the external donation platform.

## Timing of Service Provision

The App is available for use immediately after download.

## Returns and Cancellation

As there is no paid sale, there is no transaction subject to return or cancellation. The handling of voluntary donations follows the rules of the external donation platform.
