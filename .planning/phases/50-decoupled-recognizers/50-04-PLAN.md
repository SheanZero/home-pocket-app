Evolve `VoiceCategoryResolver` into `CategoryRecognizer` by deleting its step-1 merchant lookup and the `_merchantDatabase` dependency, then move it into `lib/application/voice/recognition/`. DECOUP-02 requires it to resolve L2 from keywords even with no merchant. This is a mechanical extraction of already-tested code: ~80% deletion + rename, per RESEARCH. The full-L2 seed (Plan 02) is what gives this engine its expanded reach; this plan is the engine shape.
Output: `CategoryRecognizer` in the recognition directory + ported tests; old `VoiceCategoryResolver` file removed (its provider is rewired in Plan 05).
