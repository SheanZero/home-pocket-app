**CR-01 sync-ingestion partial-triple — NON-ISSUE.** `transaction_sync_mapper.dart:48-69` validates the full triple inline at the wire boundary (presence + `originalAmount>0` + ISO-4217 + valid rate) and degrades partial/invalid triples to JPY-native (all-null) rather than persisting them.
