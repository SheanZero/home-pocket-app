# Sketch 004: Home + Palette Redesign

## Design Question

Which overall visual identity best balances monthly clarity, the Daily/Joy dual-ledger model,
and the warm emotional character of Home Pocket without becoming decorative or game-like?

## How to View

Open `.planning/sketches/004-home-palette-redesign/index.html` in a browser.

## Variants

- **A: 温润日系 · 生活手帐 ★ Selected** — warm washi canvas, pine green, muted benizakura, editorial serif headings, quiet stacked sections.
- **B: 清爽现代 · 清晰月报** — cool neutral canvas, crisp teal, flat information hierarchy, compact rows, linear Joy progress.
- **C: 温暖精致 · 情绪月刊** — plum, rose, and copper identity, expressive but restrained hero, Joy story as an editorial focal point.

## Shared Product Constraints

- Same July 2026 data and navigation in every variant.
- Primary action remains Add transaction; long-press/voice can remain an implementation detail.
- `Σ joy_contribution` is the only Joy expression; no density/ROI surfaces.
- Reaching 100% remains ambient: no celebration, badge, confetti, streak, or achievement copy.
- Daily/Joy/Shared remain semantically distinct and usable in light and dark modes.

## What to Look For

Compare which version makes the monthly picture easiest to understand in five seconds, which one
makes adding a transaction feel most obvious, and which color identity feels durable enough to
carry the rest of the app.
